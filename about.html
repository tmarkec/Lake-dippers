About us

Group "Lake dippers" started more than 10 years ago through anual event which is held on St.Stephens day (26th of December).Local people from Killarney would meet at Dundag beach in National Park Killarney and bravely dug into freezing cold water. There was also some marathon swimm events during summer months. Idea behind it was to promote healthy aspect of life, and to build stronger community in Killarney. Over years our group gained more than 50 members who regularly on weekly basis goes for swimm no matter what weather conditions are. Don't be surprised if you come across group of people in the middle of the January. Most usual meeting point is Dundag Beach, but there have been days when group would meet on other lakes. You don't have to be good swimmer to join the group and to dip in the cold lake in the winter :).
