About us

Group "Lake dippers" started more than 10 years ago through anual event which is held on St.Stephens day (26th of December).Local people from Killarney would meet at Dundag beach in National Park Killarney and bravely dug into freezing cold water. Through years there were marathon swimm events during summer months as well. Lake dippers promote healthy aspect of life while building community.
Over years our group gained more than 50 brave members who regularly visit lake on weekly basis. Don't be surprised if you come across group of people swimming in the lake in the middle of the January. Meeting spot for the group is usually Dundag Beach at Muckross lake which is next to the famous tourist destination Muckross House.

Benefits of swimming

Swimming is low impact sport, with huge benefits.It works your entire body from head to toe. Tone your muscles and build endurance and strenght. It improves your heart and lungs and help with recovery during injuries. It also improves your sleep and boost your moode.

Benefits of cold water

Cold water boosts up your immune system, it activates endorphins which gives you natural high. It flushes your veins & arteries by forcing blood to the surface and to warm our extremities. You also burn much calories as your hart must pump faster & stronger. So only by sitting in cold water you burn calories.
